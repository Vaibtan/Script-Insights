Title: The Third Call

Scene: A nearly empty hospital corridor after midnight.
Maya: He called again.
Dev: Unknown number?
Maya: Third time in ten minutes.
Dev: Then answer it.
Maya: The last two times no one spoke. They just played the elevator bell.

Scene: Outside the ICU doors.
Phone: Ding.
Maya: Hello?
Voice: If your father wakes up, do not let him say my name.
Maya: Who is this?
Voice: Check the visitor log from 2009.
Dev: Maya, why are you shaking?
Maya: Because my father was in prison in 2009.
Phone: Ding.
Voice: He is awake now.
