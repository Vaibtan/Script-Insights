Title: The Audit Room

Scene: A glass meeting room late at night.
Priya: Why did you ask me to stay after everyone left?
Jon: Because the numbers in quarter three do not reconcile.
Priya: Then call finance.
Jon: Finance says the override came from your login.
Priya: That is impossible.
Jon: Then explain why the server room opened at 2:14 a.m.
Priya: Because I was there deleting the transfer before the payout cleared.
Jon: You altered the ledger?
Priya: I altered the theft.
Jon: Who else knows?
Priya: The person waiting outside this room.
