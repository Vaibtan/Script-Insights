Title: The Unsent Draft

Scene: A small kitchen just before sunrise.
Leena: You are still awake.
Kabir: I was trying to delete that email.
Leena: The one you never sent?
Kabir: The one where I told you I was leaving the city.
Leena: You stayed.
Kabir: Only because your mother called before I pressed send.
Leena: You never told me that.
Kabir: I thought staying would count more than explaining.
Leena: It didn't.
Kabir: I know.
